North West Ruby User Group

NWRUG is a Manchester-based community for people interested in the Ruby programming language. We run regular events open to anyone who wants to learn more about Ruby. We also have a mailing list where you can discuss Ruby and related topics online.

Our Mission

Our aim is to help grow and support the Ruby community here in the North West. We want NWRUG to be a diverse and inclusive community that is open to all those with an interest in Ruby regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race or religion.
